1. Introduction

We are dedicated to maintaining your trust by protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency in how we handle data.

2. Information We Collect

3. How We Use Data

Your data helps us to:

We use industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption. Uploaded files are temporary and permanently removed from our servers after processing.

We do not sell or trade your personal data. However, some trusted third-party services (e.g., analytics providers) may assist in improving our services.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance functionality and analytics. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
